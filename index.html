Flip a Coin Online to Make a Decision

1. What is FlipSimu?

FlipSimu is a heads or tails coin flip simulator. You can flip a coin virtually as if flipping a real coin.

The objective of FlipSimu is to help you in decision making. Before flipping a coin, you can decide what decision to be made when either of the heads or tails is selected.

Sometimes it is hard to get a coin for doing the coin flip. As an alternative, our team has developed the FlipSimu for everyone to easy access and flip a coin online.

Our coin has slightly difference compared to conventional coin. You can change the heads and tails' texts as well as their colors.

Our team is trying to simulate the coin flipper as real as possible. Hence, the FlipSimu is integrated with an energy simulator and coin flip sound for the coin toss.

FlipSimu also has extra features which are "Test Your Intuition" and "Test Your Luck".

Besides, if you want to roll a die instead, you could checkout our Dice Roller.

2. How to Use the Coin Flipper?

Coin flip online using FlipSimu is very easy. There are two ways to flip coin.

1. You can click the coin or click the flip button to start random flipping.

2. You can long-press and release the flip button to simulate the flipping energy. The probability of heads or tails is also 50:50 as if you toss a coin hardly or softly in the real world.

3. The result of the coin flip will then be shown.

4. The result will contribute to our world and your own statistics of heads or tails probability.

5. You can also change your coin texts, images, colors, sound and quantity of coins at the settings section.

6. Besides, FlipSimu also comes with interesting / fun features in which you could test your intuition and your luck today.

3. Types of Test

There are intuition and luck tests available on this coin flip simulator.

3.1. How does Intuition Test work?

There are 10 trials in the intuition test. You have to guess the result for each flip. The more you can get from the right guess, the higher will be your intuition score. You have to choose what will be the next result before you flip.

3.2. How does Luck Test work?

You have to get the same flip results throughout the flipping, maximum at 5 flipping. The more you can get the same flips, the higher will be your luck score.

4. When to Use FlipSimu?

These are the examples of scenarios that you can use the FlipSimu.

  • Choose A if heads and choose B if tails?
  • Buy it if heads and not buy if tails?
  • Want to know my luck today.
  • Want to know my intuition accuracy today.
